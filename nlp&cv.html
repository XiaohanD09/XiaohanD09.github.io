4. Generating Stylized Image Based on Text Description

Approach step I. Combination of GANs to generate image from text

  • The keywords of the information are captured from the traditional text information, and the corresponding images are generated based on them.
  • The generated images are serialized, and a primary key is generated to uniquely identify them, and the stylized images are generated in sequence according to the primary key

    • Approach step II. One step to generate image from text

  • try to learn the mapping between image and text descriptions through the process of image to text descriptions by mapping the original text information according to the primary key.
  • After training, the direct generation of text to abstracted images is learned.
